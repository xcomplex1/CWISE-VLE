The following link will take you to a model of convection in the Earth's mantle. It shows how the convection currents form a circular pattern in the mantle and how they push on the plates.


When you click the "Show currents" button, look at the black square shown on the top of the model. Notice the direction that the currents (arrows) are moving when they are at the top of the mantle at the convergent boundary. Are the currents (arrows) moving towards each other, or away from each other?


Then look at the black square shown on the right side of the model. Look at the direction that the currents are moving when two plates are moving away from each other (divergent boundary). Are the currents (arrows) at the top of the mantle moving towards each other, or away from each other?
