How many earthquakes are there near where you live?

In the United States, there are thousands of earthquakes that happen each year that are too small even to be felt. Each year the southern California area has about 10,000 earthquakes. Most of them are so small that they are not felt. In this activity, you will find out how many earthquakes have occurred on each coast in the past several days.

The maps you will see in this activity are updated all day long. Any square on the map represents an earthquake.

Once you jump to the link below, zoom in by clicking on the location in the United States closest to where you live.
