Review a Diagram

You are now convection current experts! Two students have created a MySystem diagram to describe heat energy in the convection current demonstration video you saw earlier, but they want help improving their diagram.

Help them by reviewing their MySystem diagram and giving them suggestions in the next step.

Feedback Pointers:
  • Does the diagram show each step of energy flow between objects? Is the diagram missing any steps?
  • Does the diagram show the correct direction of energy flow?
  • Do the arrow labels have correct information?
  • Do the arrow labels have enough information?
