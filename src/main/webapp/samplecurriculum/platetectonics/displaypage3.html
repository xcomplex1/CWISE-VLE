Looking at Our Continent

Wegener was unable to convince the scientific world that his theory was true. Years later, with the addition of the work of other scientists, Wegener's theory of Continental Drift was generally accepted, and contributed to support the new Theory of Plate Tectonics.

In this project, you, like Wegener, will look for patterns in the natural world. You will then see how the Theory of Plate Tectonics can explain these patterns.
